eCourts Portal of Western Australia - Help: My Fines and Infringements

Search for and Pay a Jury Infringement

To view and pay a Jury Infringement you do not need to register an account or be logged in. 

01. From the home screen click the Pay Jury Infringement option:

  • Pay a Jury Infringement

The Pay a Jury Infringement screen displays.

02. Enter the Infringement Number and Customer Reference Number (Located on Infringement Notice or Final Demand Notice).

Details are entered.

03. Enter the captcha code.

The captcha code is entered.

04. Click Find Infringement.

The Infringement is displayed.

05. To make a payment against an Infringement, complete the Credit Card fields.

  • Card Number
  • Card Holder
  • Expiry (MM/YY)
  • CVV

06. Click Submit.

A confirmation popup appears.

07. Click OK.

The popup closes.

08. Keep the browser open whilst the payment is being processed.

Once processing is complete the Jury Infringement Summary screen displays.

09. Press Print to print a copy of the receipt (or to save to PDF).

10. Press Home to return to the eCourts Portal Home screen.
